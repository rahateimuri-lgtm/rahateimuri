## Context

UGC and influencer content usually lives as a pile of one-off deals. At Yousician I was asked to make it a **system**: something that produces on-brand, on-performance creator content predictably, month after month, instead of campaign by campaign.

## What I did

- **Designed the full pipeline:** creator sourcing → briefing → production → performance review, with each stage documented so it runs without me in the room.
- **Built the in-house studio** and tripled organic content output (**3×**) by combining staff production, UGC and influencers under one creative direction.
- **Wrote briefs that travel.** Clear, repeatable briefs let dozens of creators ship content that still feels like one brand.
- **Closed the loop with data.** Performance feeds back into sourcing and briefing, so each cycle compounds instead of resetting.
- **Layered in AI where it scales** (Superscale and others) to push output without adding headcount.

## Result

**One repeatable creator engine**, not a stack of disconnected deals, feeding Yousician and GuitarTuna a steady flow of UGC and influencer content, and a **3× lift** in in-house organic output.
