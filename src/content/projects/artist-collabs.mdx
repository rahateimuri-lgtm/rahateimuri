## Context

Working with iconic catalogues (**Paul McCartney & Wings**, **David Bowie**, **Warner Chappell Music**) means the bar is set before you start. The work is to honour the artist and the rights while making content that actually performs on a 2020s feed.

## What I did

- **Led social across the collaborations** end to end: from creative concept to the content that shipped on Yousician and GuitarTuna's channels.
- **Translated catalogue into feed-native content**, turning legendary songs into formats people learn, play and share, not just watch.
- **Held the brand and licensing line.** Official artist work carries strict guidelines; every asset stayed on-brand and on-rights while still earning attention.
- **Coordinated across stakeholders** (label, brand and creative) so a high-sensitivity collaboration moved at social speed without friction.

## Result

**Global, multi-artist campaigns delivered**: official collaborations with **Paul McCartney & Wings**, **David Bowie**, and **Warner Chappell Music** — three of music's most recognisable names, executed at the standard their catalogues demand and the cadence social rewards.
