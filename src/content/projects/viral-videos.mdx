## Context

Going viral once is luck; doing it on purpose, repeatedly, across very different briefs, is a craft. I direct short-form that spreads: both the easy-to-love travel content and the genuinely hard stuff: product-led videos for an app.

## What I did

- **Engineered for the first second.** Every video is built around a hook that earns the next three seconds, then a structure that holds retention to the end.
- **Directed end to end.** Concept, shot list, on-camera direction, edit and sound: one accountable creative line from idea to upload.
- **Made products spread.** The 50M-view travel campaign is the headline, but the harder win is product-led content (GuitarTuna) pulling 1.4M+, content that has to teach and convert, not just delight.
- **Re-shipped what worked.** Winning formats were re-cut and adapted across platforms and accounts so a hit became a series, not a fluke.

## Result

**50M+ views across platforms**, anchored by a single video at **24.7M** (≈50M cross-platform) and a **21M** Instagram Reel, alongside product-led videos engineered to convert, which is the far harder bar.
