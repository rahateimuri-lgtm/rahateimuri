Podcast Visual Branding & Thumbnail Design 🎙️🎨

Category:
Design
Client:
Makler & Vermittler Podcast

I create visual assets for podcast series, including podcast cover art, episode covers, and thumbnails. My work focused on translating audio content into clear, engaging visuals that capture attention and communicate key messages at a glance.

This includes designing quote-based graphics, where key insights or statements from episodes are turned into visually compelling content for social media. By combining strong typography, clean layouts, and consistent branding, I ensure that each design is both aesthetically appealing and easy to understand.

I develop cohesive visual systems across episodes to maintain brand recognition while allowing enough flexibility to highlight different topics and guests. The goal is to create professional, scroll-stopping designs that support audience growth, engagement, and overall podcast visibility.
