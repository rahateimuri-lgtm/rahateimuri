Graphic Design 🎨

Category:
Marketing & Content Design
Client:
AIESEC Paderborn

This project explores social media visual design for AIESEC Paderborn LC, with a focus on clear communication, consistent branding, and platform-adapted content across Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

The goal was to create visuals that feel structured, approachable, and engaging, balancing professionalism with a human, student-driven tone.

The visual direction is based on AIESEC’s brand identity, using bold colors, clean layouts, and strong contrast to ensure clarity and recognition. Each design is created to communicate purpose quickly while remaining visually consistent and accessible.

Student organisations often need to share information, recruitment messages, and event updates within very limited attention spans. This project focuses on:

The visuals work both as standalone posts and as part of a cohesive overall feed.

Instead of overdesigning or relying on trends, the focus is on making information easy to grasp and visually appealing. The content is created to support understanding, engagement, and trust within a student audience.
